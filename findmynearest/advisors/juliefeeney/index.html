Experience & Qualifications

About Time Home Loans provides financial solutions to the consumer market. With a friendly team experienced across Mortgages, Buy-to-Let Mortgages, Secured Loan Lending & Protection, perfectly placed to handle your business with expertise and efficiency.

The About Time Home Loans team is led by Julie Feeney (CeMap Qualified) who has served more than 29 years in financial services, predominantly in the Mortgage, Secured and Unsecured loan sectors. Our ‘About’ Us is more About ‘YOU’ and isn’t that ‘About Time’

Having been in this industry since 1986 I love it just as much as I did at the start, managing money has been a huge journey delivering mortgages and loans, the experience of surviving 2 recessions helping clients.

Our team believe that an excellent customer experience is essential, focusing on your needs and satisfying them. Priding ourselves on fantastic communication and efficiency we give a valuable service to YOU our customer, don’t you think it is ‘About Time’ you knew you were Number 1.

Whether YOU want a Mortgage Review to obtain the best rate available, purchase a new home for the first time or you are on the move, our sole goal is to find YOU the perfect solution, our CeMap Advisers’ are ‘Comprehensive Advisers’ and do not have the restriction of advising from one provider or a range and this gives YOU so much more to choose from.Mortgage & Protection Advice for both the Consumer and/or Businesses5 core reasons to use About Time Home Loans1.
